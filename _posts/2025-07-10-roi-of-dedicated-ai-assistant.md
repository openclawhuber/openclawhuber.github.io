"Is it worth it?" is the first question executives ask. Let me answer it with numbers.

## The Costs

**One-time setup:**
- Hardware (Mac Mini): $600, or VPS: $0
- Professional setup: $250
- Your time for onboarding: 2-3 hours

**Monthly ongoing:**
- AI API costs: $15-30 (varies by usage)
- Hosting (VPS only): $5-6
- Maintenance service: $50 (optional)
- Total: **$20-86/month**

## The Savings

Based on data from 20+ clients I've set up:

**Email management:** 5-8 hours/week saved
- Triage, drafting, follow-ups automated
- Conservative value: 5 hrs × $100/hr = $500/week

**Scheduling:** 2-4 hours/week saved
- Meeting coordination, conflict resolution, reminders
- Conservative value: 2 hrs × $100/hr = $200/week

**Research:** 2-3 hours/week saved
- Business decisions, competitive analysis, vendor comparison
- Conservative value: 2 hrs × $100/hr = $200/week

**Communication:** 1-3 hours/week saved
- Client updates, internal communication, follow-ups
- Conservative value: 1 hr × $100/hr = $100/week

**Admin:** 1-2 hours/week saved
- Data entry, document organization, expense tracking
- Conservative value: 1 hr × $100/hr = $100/week

**Total weekly savings:** 11+ hours = **$1,100/week** (conservatively)

## The ROI Calculation

| Period | Cost | Savings | Net |
|--------|------|---------|-----|
| Month 1 (setup) | $850 | $2,200 | +$1,350 |
| Month 2+ | $50 | $4,400 | +$4,350 |
| Year 1 total | $1,400 | $52,800 | +$51,400 |

**First-year ROI: 3,671%**

Even if I'm wildly optimistic and you only save 5 hours per week at $50/hour:

| Period | Cost | Savings | Net |
|--------|------|---------|-----|
| Year 1 | $1,400 | $13,000 | +$11,600 |

**Conservative ROI: 829%**

## The Intangible Benefits

Numbers don't capture everything:

- **Reduced stress** from inbox overwhelm
- **Better decisions** from faster, more thorough research
- **Improved client relationships** from consistent, timely communication
- **More focus time** for high-value strategic work
- **Work-life balance** from not checking email at midnight

## The Break-Even Point

At the conservative estimate, your AI assistant pays for itself in **the first week.** At the realistic estimate, the setup cost is recovered in **2 days.**

There is no business investment with a better ROI. Period.

## Who Should NOT Get an AI Assistant?

- Someone who processes fewer than 10 emails per day
- Someone with no recurring scheduling needs
- Someone who genuinely prefers doing everything manually
- Someone who doesn't trust technology with their information

For everyone else, the question isn't "should I?" — it's "why haven't I already?"
